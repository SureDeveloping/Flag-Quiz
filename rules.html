The Flag Quiz

How to play

In this game, a flag of a country will be displayed. Your task is to guess which country's flag it represents by clicking on the corresponding button. You have three possible answer options to choose from. If you guess correctly, you'll earn one point, and the correct answer will be revealed. To proceed to the next question or skip a question, click on "Next Question." If you wish to stop playing, click on "Quit." Enjoy the game and test your knowledge of flags from around the world!
